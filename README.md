The Barbie Movie Project

Introduction
Welcome to the Barbie Movie Adventure! Step into the captivating world of Barbieland, where Barbie and Ken are about to embark on an extraordinary journey that blends beauty, bravery, and a touch of magic. In this Python project, you'll have the chance to be a part of their story by coding the challenges they encounter along the way.

Barbieland is a place where various versions of Barbies and Kens reside, each with their own unique qualities. Among them, there are the carefree Beach Kens, the accomplished Barbies, and a group of discontinued dolls, considered outcasts due to their unconventional traits.

The adventure begins when Barbie, the epitome of beauty, suddenly faces an existential crisis. Overnight, she undergoes a transformation, experiencing unexpected challenges such as bad breath, cellulite, and flat feet, which disrupt her perfect world. It's a journey that will test her resilience and self-discovery.

Ken, who secretly yearns for a closer connection with Barbie, stows away in her convertible, determined to join her quest. Barbie, though initially reluctant, accepts his presence as they venture into the unknown. Their journey takes them from the enchanting Venice Beach to Barbieland's heart, with unexpected encounters and life-altering realizations along the way.

As they navigate this remarkable adventure, you'll code and solve three unique challenges that mirror key moments in their journey. These challenges are your opportunity to contribute to their story and help them overcome obstacles.

So, are you ready to embark on this enchanting adventure, coding the challenges that Barbie and Ken face on their path to self-discovery and societal change? Let's get started and bring their story to life!

Challenges
Beach Adventure Challenge
Help Barbie and Ken manage their expenses for a day at the beach. You'll calculate the total cost of their beach day, apply discounts, and determine each person's share of the expenses.

Escape Plan Challenge
Assist Barbie, Ken, and Gloria in devising an escape plan. You'll calculate the time required for different escape routes and determine the fastest option.

Societal Reformation Challenge
Help Barbie implement changes to promote equality and fairness in Barbieland. You'll calculate the success rate of proposed changes and determine how many more changes are needed to reach a target success rate.
